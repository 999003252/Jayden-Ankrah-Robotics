Project Overview

This project involved the design and construction of a robotic arm capable of mimicking human hand movements...

Components & Design

  • Arduino Uno/Nano: Microcontroller for servo control.
  • Servo Motors: For each joint (fingers, wrist, etc).
  • 3D Printed Parts: Custom parts for structure and hand.
  • Flex Sensors: Used for gesture input.
  • Power Supply: External reliable power.
