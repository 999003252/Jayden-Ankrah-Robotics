About Me

Hello! I'm Jayden Ankrah, a passionate and driven first-year Robotics Engineering student at the University of Connecticut. From a young age, I've been fascinated by how things work, which naturally led me to the world of robotics. My goal is to leverage my education to design and build innovative robotic systems that can solve real-world problems.

I am particularly interested in autonomous navigation, machine learning applications in robotics, and human-robot interaction. I am proficient in languages like Python and C++, and have hands-on experience with platforms like Arduino and Raspberry Pi. I'm eager to apply my skills to challenging projects and collaborate with fellow engineers.
